About Us

The Syro-Malabar Catholic Church is one of 22 Eastern (Oriental) Catholic Churches in full communion with the Vatican. It is the second largest Eastern Catholic Church after the Ukrainian Church and with 4.6 million believers, is the largest of the Saint Thomas Christian (Nazrani) denominations.
